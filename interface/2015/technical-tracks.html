Technical Tracks
Track A: System z

The System z track is mainly concerned with topics related to IBM System z hardware and operating systems, including but not limited to the following:
  • z/OS
  • z/VM
  • zLinux
Track B: Middleware

The Middleware track is mainly concerned with topics related to major middleware components built on top of IBM System z, including but not limited to the following:
  • DB2 for z/OS
  • CICS
  • IMS
  • MQ for z/OS
  • Optim
  • DB2 Tools
  • WebSphere for z/OS
  • WAS
  • Rational
  • Tivoli

Track C: Solution
The Solution track is mainly concerned with topics related to emerging solutions based on IBM System z, including but not limited to the following:

  • Cloud
  • Mobile
  • Business Analytics
  • Social
  • GDPS/Active-Active
  • Smarter City Solution
